Welcome to Storm Technologies

The home of Zoohu Search and GivMix Incentives

Founded in 2014, Storm Technologies have built up an international development team whose principal goal is to develop a new type of search - "Zoohu".

Zoohu's search platform allows users and retailers to connect more effectively and makes online shopping a more enjoyable experience. Zoohu achieves this by enabling it's userbase to easily interact with over 100,000 retailers in 65 countries. Shoppers are more likely to shop when the content is in their native language - that's why we provide support for over 40 languages, allowing our userbase to interogate multiple search engines AND multiple websites with just ONE search.

We are also developing a new employee incentive programme - "GivMix" which leverages our retail platform with a range of exclusive employee incentives, exciting rewards and CSR fulfillment from top brands. Real rewards, real motivation for YOUR team in ONE platform.
